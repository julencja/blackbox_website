Erin ("@RobotDiva":https://twitter.com/robotdiva) has over 10 year robotics experience across academia and industry. Her projects range from military casualty extraction, assistive living devices, and pioneering the video conferencing robot in her role as a Chief Product Officer for "Anybot, Inc.":https://www.anybots.com/#front Currently, as the product marketing manager for Adept Technology, she is responsible for productizing autonomous navigation software and hardware. She holds BS & MS in Mechanical and Industrial Engineering degrees form Northeastern University and the University of University of Massachusetts where she's developed a robotic hand to help people in wheelchairs open doors.
