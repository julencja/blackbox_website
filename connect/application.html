Application Process Blackbox Connect

Consider applying if:

  • You have founded a startup outside of the US
  • You have proven traction in your home market
  • You seek to establish US-based business operations or launch globally
  • You are in the process of, or seriously thinking about, raising funds for your startup
  • You want to learn how to do business the Silicon Valley way
  • You are ready to change your mindset
  • You are a doer, not a conjurer

How to Apply?

To ensure the highest value possible for all participants, not all applicants are accepted. In addition, only first 12 participants may stay at Blackbox Mansion for free. All applications must be completed and submitted here»

Stages of the Application Process

  • Online Application Questionnaire Application fee has to be paid to ensure processing of the application. If you get accepted, the fee goes towards the Program Fee, otherwise it is returned to the applicant.
  • Video Interview (via Skype)
  • Letter of Recommendation
  • Final decision

Application Questionnaire

Prepare to answer the following questions:

Company-specific Questions
  1. Company name
  2. Company website
  3. What year was the company founded?
  4. Which markets/countries does your company operate in?
  5. Describe your company’s core product in one Tweet.
  6. What is your startup’s financial stage?
    • bootstrapped
    • self-funded
    • angel/seed-funded
    • VC-funded (series A, B or beyond)
    • profitable
    • other
  7. What is your company’s business model?
  8. How does your company acquire users/customers?
  9. What metrics do you use to measure your company growth?
  10. Who are, or could become, your main competitors?
  11. What stage is your core product on?
    • an idea / a .ppt or .key presentation
    • a prototype
    • working beta
    • up and running
  12. How many founders and/or employees does your current team have?
  13. What is the composition of your company team? (How many product/engineers, business development/sales/marketing, operational and administrative?)
Founder-specific Questions
  1. First Name
  2. Last Name
  3. Country
  4. Email
  5. LinkedIn profile
  6. Facebook profile
  7. Twitter name
  8. Blog
  9. What is your role in your startup?
  10. What type of company do you want to build?
    • Build Value and do a quick flip (Groupon Clone)
    • Build a great product (37Signals / Craigslist)
    • Change the world (Google / Facebook)
  11. Why are you the best person to build this company, and not someone else?
  12. What is your biggest achievement on a professional level?
  13. List any important things you have built so far (companies, side projects, libraries, open source contributions etc.) Provide links where relevant.
  14. List any relevant conferences, events, meetups from the last 3 years you organized & or were speaking/mentoring at. When was it and how did you contribute?
  15. List any publications you authored or co-authored. Provide links where relevant.
  16. What is your main motivation to attend Blackbox Connect?
  17. How did you learn about Blackbox Connect?
  18. Please provide contact information for two referrals (colleagues, coworkers, advisors, etc.)

If you are filing an application for the team of 2 founders, both founders will have to answer the founder-specific questions.
