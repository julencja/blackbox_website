Freqently Asked Questions Blackbox Connect

Is it for everyone who has the money to pay the program fee?

No. To ensure the highest possible value for all the participants Blackbox Connect is a highly selective program. Learn more about the application process»

What does a typical day of the program look like?

Though the program agenda will be very packed, we make sure that you have time to keep your hand on the pulse of your startup and do what needs to be done. The mornings here are for you to tend to your business, have meetings, or connect with your team back home. The program schedule for talks and workshops begins each day around noon and goes through the afternoon and early evenings.

What is the venue like?

Talks, workshops and accommodation are all in the beautiful Blackbox Mansion in Atherton, just around 10 minutes car ride away from Stanford, Palo Alto and/or Sand Hill Road where other lectures and workshops will be delivered. There is a pool and jacuzzi, and California sun, so bring your swimsuit:) The participants stay in shared rooms. We always make sure that female participants have a "girls-only" room.

What if I want my own private room?

Several hotel options are available at your expense, hotel rates range from $100 to about $500 per night.

Is transportation provided?

No, but you may rent a car and parking is available for free. Public transportation such as train and buses are available, but not frequently. Taxis are available by calling them; they charge ~ $5/mile.

Is food included in the price?

Coffee, tea, light breakfast and lunch are provided each weekday. Some dinners are included at scheduled events.

I need a visa to come to the US. Will Blackbox help me obtain one?

For accepted participants, we will send a personalized formal invitation letter so you may include it with your visa application at the consulate where you apply.

When and how do I pay?

You have to pay the Application Fee of $100 ($200 if you want to participate as a team of 2 founders) through our Application Page on Eventbrite. The fee goes towards the Program Fee, and will be returned to you in case you don't get accepted to the program.

The Program Fee should be wired through bank transfer before you come to the program. We reserve the right to reject participants who don't pay before they come to Blackbox.
