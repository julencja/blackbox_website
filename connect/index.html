About the Program Blackbox Connect

Blackbox Connect is a 2-week residential program for selected international entrepreneurs who want to bring their projects to the global market. While you are here you experience the secret sauce of Silicon Valley: its people. For two weeks you will live in Blackbox Mansion and experience an intense agenda of meetings and workshops with inspiring serial entrepreneurs, investors, experts and executives who will discuss:

  • How they launched, and built successful startups in Silicon Valley
  • How to interface with the investment community and raise funds from Silicon Valley VCs
  • How to establish a co-existing US based operation for your startup
  • How to apply qualitative research frameworks in your product development
  • How to meet Silicon Valley standards with your pitch and executive summary
  • How to develop great products with lean UX
  • How to sort out US-immigration issues

The program takes place 3-4 times a year.

Workshops:

Janice Fraser CEO of LUXr Inc., Cofounder of Adaptive Path

  • Understanding your customer: Lean Persona generation, identifying assumptions about your customer and turning thstrong into hypotheses, validating these hypotheses, and understanding your customers' needs. Dump and sort methods, persona sketching, and essentials of user interviews.
  • Acting on Customer Needs: Ideation around how customers could use your product to meet their needs, validating those uses and deriving and prioritizing features from thstrong. 6-up sketching, dot voting, 2x2 prioritization grid.
  • Metrics: An introduction to qualitative vs. quantitative research. Lightweight frameworks for qualitative research.

Bill Joos Cofounder of Garage Technology Ventures (with Guy Kawasaki), Former VP of Sales at Apple
