Blackbox Connect July 30th - August 11th 2012 // Silicon Valley

Intense educational program for 12 exceptional startup founders from all around the globe

Sponsors & Partners

Follow @blackboxvc

Who can apply?

We help outstanding entrepreneurs from all around the globe bring their startups to the next, global level. We are looking for startups with proven traction in their home markets.


Learn more about the Application Process»


Consider applying if:

  • You have founded a startup outside of the US
  • You have proven traction in your home market
  • You seek to establish US-based business operations or launch globally
  • You are in the process of, or seriously thinking about, raising funds for your startup
  • You want to learn how to do business the Silicon Valley way
  • You are ready to change your mindset
  • You are a doer, not a conjurer

Why participate?

While you are here, you experience the "secret sauce" of Silicon Valley: its people. For two weeks you will live in Blackbox Mansion and experience an intense agenda of meetings and workshops with inspiring serial entrepreneurs, investors, and experts.

Learn more about Previous Programs»


You will learn:

  • How Silicon Valley entrepreneurs launched and built successful companies
  • How to interface with the investment community and raise funds from Silicon Valley VCs
  • How to establish US based operation for your startup
  • How to make your pitch & executive summary meet the Silicon Valley standards
  • How to develop great products with lean UX
