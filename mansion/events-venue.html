Blackbox Mansion is a great venue for events.

We've been hosting multiple dinner parties, meetups, barbecues or retreats for entrepreneurs, hackers, and VCs from all around the globe. If you are intested in organizing an event for entrepreneurs at Blackbox Mansion, contact us with the details.

Robot Launchpad Retreat April 2012

For two weekend days the Robot Launchpad community or robotics experts and amateurs gathered together at Blackbox Mansion to learn how to build startups in robotics.

Hacker Dojo Fundraiser April 2012

The Fundraiser was organized to help Hacker Dojo raise funds to get compliant with the City of Mountain View fire regulations. David Weekly gave an inspiring talk about building the Dojo community.

Bay Area LISP Meetup February 2012

The event organized by Bay Area LISP hacker meetup included a series of lightning talks by hacker members of the community and a BBQ party.

ScaleIT Pitch Day June 2011

20+ Danish entrepreneurs brought to Silicon Valley by Scale IT pitched their startups during the Pitch Day Party. Watch them pitching on Vimeo.
