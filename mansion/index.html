Blackbox Mansion is an exclusive live+work space for entrepreneurs in the heart of Silicon Valley.

We host regular events and workshops with serial entrepreneurs, experts and investors. If you are an entrepreneur visiting Silicon Valley, Blackbox Mansion is the best place for you to stay and make your first connections.

‹ ›

Blackbox Mansion is located in Atherton, just around 10 minutes away from Stanford, Palo Alto and Sandhill Road by car/bike/public transport.

In order to stay in Blackbox Mansion, you need to book it through AirBnB. We will accept only entrepreneurs who actually work on something. Priority is given to people referred by our Referral Partners or Blackbox Ambassadors.

International Feel

I had a really good time staying at the Blackbox Mansion. It has a real international feel - as well as several people from across the United States, there were people from Spain, Denmark, Romania, Australia and South Africa. The house is really nice, with dedicated office where you can work in peace, and everyone's really friendly and social.

It's great to be able to bounce ideas off and discuss problems with people who are doing the same thing. Definitely a great place to stay if you're coming to the Bay Area to launch your startup.

Jack London, United Kingdom

Meeting New People

Fadi was a great host and I truly enjoyed the experience at the house. It is centrally located to easily get to Palo Alto and have meetings with people in the area, however also close to the CalTrain to get into SF. Meeting new people at the house is the primary benefit as everyone is working on interesting projects and can help you think through different aspects of your business and also highlight things about the "startup scene" that you might not be aware of. Great place to stay!

Francisco New York, United States

Brilliant Space

As a startup founder, I found Blackbox Mansion to be a brilliant living and co-working space in the Valley. It had a homely feel, fantastic people (mostly other founders), and had plenty of places to work or relax. I ended up extending my initial 7 day booking quite significantly to soak up more of the unique Silicon Valley lifestyle that Blackbox Mansion delivers.

Whilst the location is best suited to those who have a car, if you're not afraid of walking or bussing it to the Caltrain, you can easily get up to San Francisco or to the other locations of the Valley.

Brett Australia
